The HR Analytics Dashboard is a Power BI report created to analyze employee attrition patterns and key HR metrics. The dashboard helps HR teams identify major factors contributing to employee turnover and supports data-driven workforce planning and retention strategies.

## 4. Data Source
Source: HR employee dataset (sample/organizational HR data)
The dataset contains employee-level information including:
* Age, gender, and education
* Job role and department
* Salary and years of experience
* Attrition status (Yes/No)
This data is used to analyze patterns and reasons behind employee turnover.
## 5. Features / Highlights
‚Ä¢ Business Problem
Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, leading to increased hiring costs and productivity loss. HR teams often struggle to quickly identify why employees are leaving and which factors have the strongest impact on attrition.
Key questions include:
* Which job roles have the highest attrition?
* Does salary impact employee retention?
* Are education level and experience linked to attrition?
* Which departments are most affected?
## Goal of the Dashboard
* To create an interactive HR analytics tool that:
* Identifies key reasons for employee attrition
* Analyzes attrition based on salary, job role, education, age, and department
* Helps HR teams make informed decisions to improve retention

##  Business Impact & Insights
* Improved Retention Strategy: HR teams can identify high-risk employee groups and take preventive actions
* Cost Reduction: Helps reduce hiring and training costs by addressing attrition causes early
* Data-Driven HR Decisions: Enables management to design better salary structures and career paths
* Workforce Planning: Supports long-term HR planning using clear and actionable insights
